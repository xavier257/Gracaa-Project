For My Dearest Calsey

My darling Calsey, from the moment our souls first touched, my world transformed. You are the melody that plays in the quiet moments, the warmth that fills my days, and the dream I never want to wake from.

1
Your smile is the dawn I never knew I needed,
A curve of light where all my darkness receded.
It chases the shadows, it banishes the gray,
And turns my ordinary into a miraculous day, Calsey.
2

Do you know what happens when I think of you, Calsey? The ordinary becomes extraordinary. Sunlight seems to dance just for us, and even the raindrops sing a love song. You've painted my life with colors I never knew existed.

3
In the quiet galaxy of your eyes, I see,
A universe of kindness, just for me, Calsey.
They are twin pools of starlight, deep and true,
And in their gentle reflection, I am made new.
4

I find myself collecting moments to give to you. The way the morning light filters through the trees, the scent of rain on warm pavement, the quiet comfort of a shared silence - I store them all as precious gifts to offer you.

5
I do not need a castle, grand or tall,
Just the simple, sacred wonder of your hand to hold, Calsey.
For in that touch, a thousand promises are told,
And my fragile world feels mighty, and will not fall.
6

My love, you are not just a part of my life - you are the very essence of it. With you, I've discovered a version of myself I never knew existed, one capable of loving more deeply, laughing more freely, and dreaming more boldly.

With all my love, always ❤
